Detailed elevation data were collected with airborne Lidar technology for the entire area of Rhode Island between April 22 and May 6, 2011. Rhode Island's data collection was a component of the larger Northeast Lidar Project. https://www.rigis.org/pages/2011-statewide-lidar-utm~b42dbcd7583f417682a7cf4c6122e0b3
